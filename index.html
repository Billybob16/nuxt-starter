Welcome to RJL Commercial Fencing & Gates, Victoria’s leading experts in commercial, industrial, and high-security fencing solutions.

Since 1980, RJL Commercial Fencing & Gates has set the benchmark as Melbourne’s trusted, family-owned provider of automatic gates, boom gates, turnstiles, and commercial security solutions. With over 40 years of industry experience, we specialise in delivering innovative design, expert craftsmanship, and customised systems built for Melbourne businesses. Every solution we install is rigorously tested to guarantee long-term reliability, outstanding performance, and uncompromising safety.

Over the past 40+ years, RJL Commercial Fencing & Gates has built a reputation as the trusted provider of security fencing and automated gate solutions for some of Melbourne’s most respected organisations. We are proud to work with a wide range of clients across industries including government, transport, education, healthcare, retail, and industrial sectors.

Our portfolio includes ongoing projects with Victoria Police, where we deliver reliable, high-security fencing systems tailored to critical infrastructure needs. At Melbourne Airport, we support large-scale operations with boom gates, turnstiles, and access control systems designed for efficiency and safety. We have also partnered with major events such as the Australian Open, ensuring secure, controlled environments for both staff and patrons.

Beyond high-profile clients, we maintain strong relationships with builders, developers, councils, schools, and aged-care providers such as Uniting AgeWell, supplying fencing solutions that balance durability, safety, and aesthetics. Companies like Hertz Australia continue to choose us for our ability to deliver projects on time and with unmatched attention to detail.

What sets us apart is our ability to adapt to each client’s unique requirements — from a single commercial sliding gate for a warehouse, to complex, fully integrated security systems for large facilities. Our proven track record and long-standing client partnerships are a testament to the quality, professionalism, and reliability that underpin every project we undertake.
